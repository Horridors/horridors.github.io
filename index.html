    SUBLEVEL
    Level 2 — The Flooded Sublevel
    The elevator clunks to a stop. Water laps at the door.
    Something green is bonking the glass of a nearby tank.
    Maybe it wants to help.
    How to escape:
    1. Find Inky Bin — search the big tank in the Aquarium (left of spawn).
    2. Find the pipe order (hub board + aquarium notes) and use the Pipe Sequencer.
    3. Collect 3 valve handles — one in each room.
    4. Read 3 gauges — red, blue, yellow (values change each run).
    5. Open the Control Panel in the Control Room and dial in the readings.
    6. Climb the exit stairs that appear in the hub.
    The Drip is faster in darkness — but Inky Bin scares him off. Stay together.

    You climb back to the corridor. Red emergency lights pulse.

    a tattered orange shape with one big scribbled eye.
    💡 Why is it so dark? Every bulb is smashed. Ex Preshon hates light — he broke them all before you got here. Your flashlight is your only friend.
    Meet EX PRESHON — the Orange Watcher
    • Stare at him (face him, keep flashlight on): he FREEZES.
    • Look away (turn your back): he CHARGES.
    • Blink too long and he's inches from your face.
    How to escape Level 3:
    1. Find 3 fuses hidden in the rooms (search carefully).
    2. Solve the Reflection Puzzle in the Library (mirror symbols).
    3. Crack the 4-digit exit code from fuse-box clues.
    4. Enter the code at the corridor exit.

    Level 4 — Two Shadows, One Way Out
    The cellar door creaks open. Stone walls. Cold air.
    Two of them down here now — Ex Preshon roams the halls,
    and his girlfriend EXLENA — the daycare teacher, pink and horned, afraid of keys.
    How to escape Level 4:
    1. Collect 4 SKELETON KEYS hidden across three chambers.
    2. Light the 3 candle sconces in the right order (clue: the candle-note shows the order).
    3. Reach the FURNACE HATCH at the bottom — it unlocks once keys + candles are done.
    4. Grab STAR TOKENS for bonus score (15 scattered).
    The monsters:
    • Ex Preshon — same rules. Torch scares him off.
    • EXLENA — the teacher. Torch doesn't scare her. But HOLDING A KEY makes her back off.
